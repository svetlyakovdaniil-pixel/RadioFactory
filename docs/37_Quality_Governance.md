# Quality Governance

## Purpose
Defines quality standards across RadioFactory.

## Scope
Code, documentation, infrastructure, UI and operations.

## Principles
Consistency, automation, measurable quality and continuous improvement.

## Quality Gates
Linting, testing, reviews and validation before release.

## Metrics
Coverage, reliability, defect rate and operational stability.

## Reviews
Regular technical and architectural reviews.

## Continuous Improvement
Quality metrics drive future improvements.

## Final Constraints
Quality Governance Version 1 complete.
